# EduAidAfrica :mortar_board:

![EduAidAfrica Landing Page](./client/src/assets/eduaid.png)

In this project, we aim to provide educational opportunities for individuals in Africa at all levels. Whether you are a student looking for scholarships :mortar_board:, a teacher searching for teaching resources, or an organization seeking to support education initiatives, EduAidAfrica is your gateway to educational opportunities.
